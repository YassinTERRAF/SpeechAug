Clean speech
   |
   |-- add noise at target SNR --------------------> noisy speech
   |
   |-- convolve with speech RIR -------------------> reverberant speech
   |
   |-- convolve speech with speech RIR
       convolve environmental noise with noise RIR
       add at target SNR --------------------------> noisy-reverberant speech

For AWGN:
   noisy-reverberant = reverberant speech + synthetic AWGN at target SNR
